# Assumptions

## Purpose

This document captures assumptions, constraints, and open questions that influence the project.

Assumptions should be reviewed regularly and updated if circumstances change.

---

## Current Assumptions

### Project Timeline

- The final proof-of-concept presentation will take place on 05 July 2026.
- Team members can contribute throughout the workshop period.
- Major scope changes will be avoided after feature freeze.

### Data and Content

- Sample inputs used for the PoC will be non-confidential.
- Sample release information will be representative of real-world scenarios.
- No sensitive customer information will be stored in the repository.

### Technology

- Claude will be the primary AI platform used for demonstrations.
- Commands, Skills, Agents, Marketplace concepts, Plugins, and MCP will be explored as part of the solution.
- Some integrations may be demonstrated conceptually rather than fully implemented.

### Team Collaboration

- The Git repository is the official source of truth.
- Important decisions will be documented in the repository.
- Team members will update assigned work regularly.
- Repository artifacts should be sufficient for another team member to understand and reproduce the PoC.

### Proof of Concept

- The objective is to demonstrate workflow automation capabilities, not a production-ready solution.
- Human review remains part of the workflow.
- Success will be measured by demonstration value and learning outcomes rather than deployment readiness.
